Hideo Kojima


Hideo Kojima, born on August 24, 1963, in Setagaya, Tokyo, Japan, is a highly influential and acclaimed video game designer, director, producer, and writer. He is best known for his work on the "Metal Gear" series, which is celebrated for its innovative approach to the stealth game genre and its complex, narrative-driven storytelling.
